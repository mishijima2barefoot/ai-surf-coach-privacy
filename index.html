Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Safety Disclaimer: AI-generated coaching advice is for informational purposes only. Surfing is a high-risk activity. Always consult local safety guidelines and professional instructors.

This Privacy Policy describes how MUTSUO ISHIJIMA ("we," "us," or "our") collects and uses your data when you use the AI Surf Coach application.

1. Data Collection

We collect text prompts and photos you upload for analysis. This data is used solely to provide personalized surfing feedback and technique analysis.

2. Third-Party AI Processing

To provide coaching insights, your inputs are transmitted to Google Gemini AI via an encrypted API. We confirm that Google Gemini AI provides the same or equal protection of your personal data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Data is shared solely for the purpose of generating real-time surfing technique analysis and personalized training advice. User inputs are processed securely and are not retained by the AI service after the coaching response is generated.

Google processes this data according to their enterprise privacy standards and does not use your personal content to train their base models without explicit consent.

3. Your Rights and Choices

You may withdraw your consent for AI data processing at any time through the app's Settings menu.

If you decline or withdraw consent, AI-powered coaching features will be disabled, though you may continue to use the manual notebook features of the app.

To request the deletion of any previously processed session data, please contact us via the support link above.
